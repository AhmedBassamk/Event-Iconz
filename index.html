About Us

EVENT ICONZ

At Event Iconz we strive to connect our models to luxury global fashions brands. We have developed into a leading model agency with the emphasis on many worldwide model image jobs ranging from fashion shows to red carpet movie premiere events. We have access to worldwide connections and if you want to travel within your jobs then we are the perfect company for you. We have a successful track record of finding a developing raw talent.
